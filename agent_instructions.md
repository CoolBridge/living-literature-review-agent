# Example Use Case

## User Input
"What is the evidence linking climate variability to malaria transmission in West Africa?"

## Agent Output (Summary)
- Research scope defined across public health, climate science, and epidemiology
- Top 10 publications ranked by relevance and rigor
- Key findings synthesized across observational and modeling studies
- Evidence gaps identified in sub-national longitudinal data
- Additional evidence available upon request (up to 50 studies)

This demonstrates structured reasoning, evidence ranking, and policy-relevant synthesis.
